About TyreMaster

Your trusted source for accurate tyre specifications for cars, bikes, and scooters.

Our Mission

TyreMaster was created to solve a common problem faced by vehicle owners, mechanics, and tyre shop professionals: finding accurate tyre size information quickly and easily.

What We Provide

Comprehensive Database

Accurate tyre sizes for popular vehicle models

Easy Search

Search by vehicle or tyre size

Mobile Friendly

Access from any device, anywhere

Our Data Sources

We gather information from:

  • Vehicle manufacturer specifications
  • Tyre manufacturer catalogs
  • Vehicle owner manuals
  • Verified user submissions
Important Notice

While we strive for accuracy, tyre specifications should always be verified with your vehicle's owner manual or a certified tyre professional. TyreMaster is for reference purposes only.
